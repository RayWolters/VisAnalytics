Orhan Strum

Chief Operating Officer

Mr. Strum serves as the Chief Operating Officer for GASTech. He works
with a wide variety of stakeholders and experts to ensure effective
management of external affairs for GASTech. Mr. Strum works closely with
officials from Tethys and Kronos to ensure a cooperative and compliant
atmosphere exists between all parties.

Prior to joining GASTech, he worked as the Chief Operating Officer for a
large multi-national corporation ensuring smooth operations across
national boundaries.
